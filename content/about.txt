Lan Ha Vu Minh is a luxury fashion label based in London, specializing in corsetry and evening wear. Founded by designer Lan Ha Vu Minh, who graduated from the London College of Fashion, the brand is defined by its dark yet dreamy aesthetic. Combining elements of Rococo with a modern, experimental edge, her designs fuse contrasting ideas, blending unconventional textures and silhouettes to create a truly unique vision. With experience in couture, Lan takes immense pride in her craftsmanship, employing couture techniques to ensure the highest level of artistry and attention to detail in every piece.
